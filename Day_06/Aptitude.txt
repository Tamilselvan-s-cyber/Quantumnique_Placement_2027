BLOCK 1 – APTITUDE (Permutation & Combination)

Ques-1

In how many ways can 3 students be selected from 7 students?


Solution:

        7
         C  = 7 ! / 3 ! . 4 !  = 35 
          3

Answer: 35 .


Ques-2

How many 2-digit numbers can be formed using digits 1, 2, 3, 4 without repetition?

Solution:

        1 st digit = 4 ways
        2 nd digit = 3 ways

        4 * 3 = 12 

Answer: 12 .



Ques-3

Solution:


        6
         P = 6 * 5 = 30 
          2
